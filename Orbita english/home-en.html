About us

Orbita Dooel Vinica has been in the market for 21 years. We manufacture corrugated sheets with tray ribs and a tile-shaped form, gutters, lamellas, gutter brackets, and facings from high-quality plasticized sheets of our own import. Orbita also produces steel structures, fences, and sells profiles and sheets wholesale and retail at the most favorable prices.
